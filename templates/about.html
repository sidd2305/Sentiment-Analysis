How it Works?



The journal sentiment analysis website utilizes the NLTK library to analyze users' journal entries, determining whether they're expressing happiness or sadness. Upon submission, the system offers tailored podcast recommendations aligned with the user's mood, ensuring a personalized listening experience. Complementing this, the site features a chatbot named Cappy, facilitating supportive conversations and providing resources for mental well-being. Additionally, users can track their emotional journey through visualizations, offering insights into their mood fluctuations over time. This integrated approach empowers users to reflect on their emotions, discover relevant content, and receive guidance and support, fostering a proactive approach to emotional wellness.
